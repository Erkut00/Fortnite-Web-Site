In November 3rd, 2023 we go back to chapter 1 map and lootpool for one month. And it was the best thing since chapter 3 season 1.

Jones used the Time Machine to travel in the past of an Alternative Timeline of Reality Zero at the beginning of Season OG for an extraction mission. However this past seems to have been already altered by Thorne's time travels as there are notable differences and anachronisms, a sign of several time travels. Certain iconic characters don't exist and their roles are filled by other characters.

Other elements have subtly changed and anachronisms are present but any change in the past of this Reality similar to Reality Zero will affect the future of the Omniverse, so what does the future hold? Will Jones succeed in his mission to save the inhabitants of Reality Zero from The Island's collapse?

Jones, after using the Time Machine receives messages from Slone. She informs Jones that he isn't there to change the past but to do an extraction, and Slone tells Jones that whatever Jones does will ripple forward to Slone's exact moment in the present. Slone sends another message to Jones, but she doesn't remember if she sent a message before or not. The Past changes even if Jones didn't mean for that to happen, The Cube does not implode, instead staying above Loot Lake where the Zero Point is located. More changes have also happened with the Ice Moon coming onto the island much earlier than originally, but this time The Ice King never arrived on the Island, meaning The Prisoner is also absent, which prevents him from creating the Volcano along with the Jungle on the northeastern side of The Island, although The Devourer had already broke out from the Ice Moon chunk. Slone sends another message to Jones and she tells Jones that they should say goodbye to the current versions of themselves, as they're heading into the unknown. STORYLINE

In May 1 2018 Season 4 started.

In Season 4 theme was superheroes and this season introduced lore in game.

As the season began, the meteor from last season, as well as many meteor fragments, had finally crashed onto the island and damaging several areas on the island. The largest fragment crashed into the Factory and Dusty Depot, resulting in Dusty Divot. The smaller fragments landed around the island, hitting Tilted Towers, the Prison, Risky Reels, and the Motel.

The meteor left behind rocks in the crater of dusty divot there rocks grant players low gravity effect when consumed.

As time went on, The Government began filling in smaller craters and construction crews started rebuilding destroyed parts of Risky Reels and Tilted Towers. At the same time, the glowing rock in Dusty Divot began to crack and open, revealing a mysterious pod containing The Visitor, later revealed to be a member of The Seven. The Visitor broke out of the pod and trashed the research facility, and after escaping to the Villain Lair, transformed the fake rocket from the superhero movie into a real rocket by using Hop Rocks stolen from The Government.

After all of that a hologram appeared in the villian lair which projected a three-day countdown for the Blast Off event on June 27th, 2018 and a siren began alarming at the Villain Lair which could be heard from everywhere on the island.

Once the countdown ended the rocket pilotted by the Visitor took off from the Villain Lair.And ascended to the sky. It stopped mid air activated a laser that pointed to Tilted Towers and boosted towards the location.Homewer before the rocket hit, The Visitor activated a device which created a tear through space-time called a rift. The rocket entered the Rift and exited from one over Moisty Mire, flying across the island before entering another Rift near Snobby Shores. Finally, the rocket exited from a Rift at Loot Lake and accelerated into the sky, creating a giant Rift in the sky, known as a crack in the sky.

While the previous rifts created by the rocket closed, the crack in the sky did not, appearing to be permanent. As time went on, the crack in the sky began to crack more and more, causing space-time around the island to destabilize. As the season was coming to a close, small rifts began appearing around the island. Rifting away the objects and objects were also rifted onto the island from diffrent realities.

STORYLINE

In December 6 2018 Season 7 started.

Season 7 started a new storyline, separate from the storyline that had been happening since Season 3. Within the next few days following the launch of Season 7, Polar Peak began to thaw out, revealing a giant castle owned by The Ice King and several surrounding buildings.

Upon realizing that the Ice in which The Ice King was frozen in was thawing, the Ice King conjured an Ice Storm on the Island to keep it cold enough to stop it from further thawing. However, several days later the snow and the ice in the Island starts melting, and with it the Iceberg continues to melt as well, and as the ice continued to melt even further, a throne room, library, and a dungeon were revealed, and in one of the rooms, there was a table with 5 symbols, each representing a King, the chair behind the fire symbol was flipped.

The dungeon contained cells, one of which contained five mysterious eggs. A further room was also revealed, housing a large mound of ice surrounded with chains.

As the days passed, the ice with chains in the Polar Peak dungeon started melting, revealing a pair of arms tied to those chains. Eventually, the ice thawed and a being known as The Prisoner had escaped, with the purpose of regaining his power. As The Prisoner regained his power, the deadlocks around his body started falling and fire appeared around his body, causing seismic activities to start happening in the Island.

The Prisoner's activities was affecting the Island, causing dozens of fissures to open up around the island. The largest one of the fissures occurred inside Tilted Towers, destroying the news station that was just constructed. In the final days of Season 7, Wailing Woods became dried up and a washed up brown color, as ashes and smoke started to rise up from the ground.

STORYLINE

In February 28 2019 Season 8 started.

Season 8 continues the Ice King storyline that began in Season 7, with The Prisoner creating a Volcano and a Jungle biome, destroying Wailing Woods and Lazy Links in the process was completely destroyed, while Tomato Temple became an unnamed POI.

Sometime after the Season had started, an Helicopter started going around the Island as if it was looking for something and, at the same time, The Unnamed Investigation Agency returned and set up a small dig-sites near Fatal Fields, inside Dusty Divot and near Loot Lake, where they found some sort of metal door. The Helicopter then, landed inside Loot Lake and 10 excavators were deployed over to dig in Loot Lake and they seemingly found what they were looking for. What the excavators unearthed was The Vault, a large and mysterious metal hatch surrounded by five big locks with metal slots.

Several days later, a holographic image of a glyph appeared above one of the vault locks. Around the same time, a large metallic floating 'key' appeared northeast of Lucky Landing, with the exact same glyph above it as the one at The Vault. It was during this time period, that players realized that the key must be inserted into The Vault slot actively projecting the glyph, and the glyphs themselves depicted what must be done to insert the key. In this instance, players must hit it with their pickaxes in order to move the vault. It took some time and effort, but the key eventually reached The Vault and slid into its hatch, activating the lock. And by the time all the keys were inserted, lava was violently spewing out of the volcano with the ground vigorously shaking in its vicinity.

A 24-hour countdown for The Unvaulting Event appeared on May 3rd 2019.

Once the countdown reached zero, hatch opened up revealing it to be a gateway to The In-Between, the dimension of The Zero Point, a mysterious nexus sphere from which all reality seemingly sprang, used to maintain The Loop that the island's inhabitants find themselves in. Surrounding the Players, six pillars slid up holding the following six items: Drum Gun, Tactical Submachine Gun, Bouncer, X-4 Stormwing, Infinity Blade, and Grappler.Players had to vote for the item that will be unvaulted by pickaxing its respective pillar. In the end, the winner was the Drum Gun.

the Drum Gun broke out of the pillar and was launched upwards out of The Vault. The Zero Point then started to glow and shine, as all players levitated upwards and flew back out of the vault.All of this activity caused The Volcano to erupt. A giant column of volcanic ash rose up from it, blocking the sunlight, and covering the entire map in darkness. The Volcano launched seven volcanic rocks, one hitting Retail Row, one hitting the Polar Peak, and five hitting Tilted Towers respectively.

Chapter 1 Season 8 ended in May 8th 2019.

STORYLINE

In May 9th 2019 Season 9 started.

The impact of the Volcano's volcanic rock to the Polar Peak seemed minor at first, but as time passed the damage became ever increasing to the integrity of the mountain, and awakened something big inside of it. Over the course of the next few weeks, the ice at Polar Peak began cracking apart and melting even further, eventually revealing a giant eye belonging to a monster known as "The Devourer", a powerful monster locked away and frozen by The Ice King.

After a few weeks, the starving Devourer broke free from the Polar Peak and went to the ocean with the Polar Peak castle on it's back and started swimming around the Island, returning to the Island every now and then, eating buildings from Pleasant Park and Loot Lake to try and satiate it's hunger.

Following these destructive events, Singularity partnered with the Imagined Order to use Pressure Plant to construct the Mecha Team Leader to fight the monster. Pressure Plant was soon taken apart, and a new layout built for construction of the Mecha. Meanwhile, around the island, holographic propoganda signs began appearing in areas to help promote support for the mecha construction.

Mecha Team Leader finally finished construction at Pressure Plant after two weeks of building. The robot was painted in a range of different colours, as a countdown for The Final Showdown event appeared, an event that would end in victory, or the complete destruction of the island.
