STORYLINE

In December 3rd 2023 Chapter 5 Season 1 started.

The Big Bang created multiple new galaxies with one of them containing a new world where the inhabitants of Asteria could take refuge. Sometime later in that new world, Jones became a member of The Underground faction after learning that Peely had been kidnapped by The Society.

After The Big Bang occurred The Society was formed by Montague. The five leaders of The Society are: Montague, Nisha, Peter Griffin, Oscar, and Valeria who is the most notorious of the five. Later The Underground was formed by Hope, Valeria's younger sister, to defeat the Society. Valeria then kidnaps Peely in order to lure Jones in order to try to get information on Pandora's Box from Jones. However this backfired as Jones, now Vengeance Jones, had no information on Pandora's Box and decided to join The Underground in order to rescue Peely.

Later in the Takedown Quests Jones, Hope, and the Looper work together to defeat The Society once and for all. The Underground succeed and Jones rescues Peely from Fencing Fields. Near the end of the season Valeria sets up a camp in Ruined Reels to try to find Pandora's Box. A character called Odyssey comes to the Island to find the Mosaic saying that the Mosaic is the key to what is coming.

In Odyssey Quests the Looper uncovers the Mosaic revealing that it is a picture of Cerberus, Hades's three-headed guard dog, from Greek mythology. Odyssey states that she can feel an ancient power growing on the Island. She also states that the Society Medallions are fragments of this ancient power. Later Earthquakes start happening near Ruined Reels. Finally on March 2nd 2024 the Titan Hand comes out of the Island holding Pandora's Box in 6 chains. The Loopers work together to break the chains and then on March 3rd Pandora's Box opens and falls to the ground unleashing the souls of The Underworld to the Island. Valeria later goes missing.

STORYLINE

In March 8th 2024 Chapter 5 Season 2 started.

The Society's remnants, Nisha, Kavel, and Montague, battle against Zeus losing in the process marking the end of The Society. Faced with the threat of Zeus, wishing to annihilate all mortals, The Oracle made a new prophecy stating "Zeus's wrath is upon you. And doom is on the horizon. You must prove the worth of humanity to Zeus and the guardians of Olympus... Before the skies turn black..." . The Looper later challenges Hades and Ares, triggering a fight against Cerberus who was defeated, then later against Ares who was also defeated, which definitively signed the declaration of war between The Looper and the Gods. The Oracle then made another prophecy stating "Darkened skies...Choking dust... Hope lost, and found again..."

Meanwhile in The Underworld, Midas was chained in a prison cell by Hades. Midas later broke out as Ascendant Midas and reunited with his crew. Together they escaped the Island with The Marigold, Midas' yacht. Jules, Midas' daughter, also made gauntlets out of Underworld Essence to control the Golden Touch. An iceberg later arrives at Helios with Aang and his sky bison Appa trapped inside leading up to the Avatar: The Last Airbender collaboration.

In Medusa Snapshot Quests the Looper retrieves Medusa's artifacts from Zeus and gives them back to Medusa. Later the Looper destroys Zeus's hourglasses, weakening Zeus. Finally, the Looper destroys Zeus's Stormforged Hourglass, defeating Zeus once and for all. The Oracle than reveals that her previous prophecy about darkened skies and choking dusts was actually talking about The Wanderer, and that Zeus was just the beginning. She quotes "You have won a great victory for humanity. But your war has not yet begun. Storm clouds gather. The Wanderer draws near. Even in the darkness... Hope will guide you." Hope also manages a new rule of freedom of Olympus.

Near the end of the season, a Mini-Event takes place called the Sandstorm Event. In the event, storm clouds gathered around the statue at Mount Olympus and the statue's eyes glowed and threw a lightning bolt at Pandora's Box using his sword. The box's orb charged up one-thirds of the way and the green water around the box turned yellow because of the boiling heat from the lightning bolt. The next day, the statue struck another lightning bolt at Pandora's Box, filling the orb up two-thirds of the way.

The next day, the statue threw a red lightning bolt at the box, imploding it. Something came out of the box, most likely The Wanderer, which bounced around to another island, creating a sandstorm that got closer and closer to the Island. It also speaks a message to us in morse code, saying "NO HOPE", "GET REKT", "WILDNESS, and "CASSATION". Eventually we can see a shark-shaped boat in the distance, which is owned by the Wasteland Warriors. The sandstorm then engulfs the southern part of the Island, starting the story for the next season...

STORYLINE

In May 24th 2024 Chapter 5 Season 3 started.

Megalo Don, attracted to the power of Pandora's Box, has conquered the southern part of the Island. He plans is destroy Helios once his ship, the Leviathan, is refueled by Nitro. A substance that affects players and vehicles which was originally from Pandora's Box. He then asked The Machinist to build a Nitro refinery (Redline Rig) to build a pipeline connected to the Leviathan ship in order to refuel it with Nitro. He also has taken all the Nitro for himself and some wastelanders have opposed him for this. Also, a mysterious hooded figure can be found in a form of graffiti and on a loading screen, most likely being The Wanderer, who The Oracle warned us about at the end of Chapter 5: Season 2 Story Quests. In Part 1 of Story Quests, "Welcome to the Wasteland", Hope and Jones introduce The Looper to the Wasteland. Hope says that Megalo Don might be strong enough to take over the Island and has The Looper test out features of the wasteland. At the end of Part 1 of Story Quests, Hope reveals that from the surveillance devices they put at Wasteland locations, she's heard some bad rumors and hopes that it's just chatter.

In Part 2, "Raiding the Rig", Hope sends The Looper to speak with Rust and Brite Raider to investigate rumors about Megalo Don's plan. Both of the two say that Megalo Don is planning something very secretive and that more information could be found at Redline Rig. After taking out one of the guards and using their keycard to upload data to Hope, Jones tells that The Machinist is Megalo Don's only competent engineer and she is splitting her time between building the pipeline and defending the refinery from intruders. Jones tasks The Looper to sabotage Megalo Don's ship. With that complete, Hope contacts The Looper, saying that Pandora's Box is still there, and asks to steal one of Pandora's Box's gems from Brutal Beachhead. Jones disagrees with Hope, saying that she shouldn't be so obsessed with Pandora's Box as he was obsessed with the Zero Point to not lose everything as he lost, but she doesn't take his advice. At the end of Part 2 of Story Quests, The Looper brings the first Pandora Gem to Hope. After taking it, she tells that her head started pounding. Meanwhile, the Island's inhabitants see a hooded figure watching them from the distance. In one of Megalo Don's transmissions on the radio he confirms that this person is The Wanderer and that they are working together. He says to not try to see The Wanderer's face as their puny minds could not comprehend it and that not even Megalo Don has seen his face. There are also mini Rift Beacons on the map that are trying to scan for The Zero Point and Backbone, Power Chord, Riot, and Ruckus founded a faction named Chiara's Chums and they have all collectively agreed on Chiara, who has yet to accept, being their leader.

In Part 3, "Enter the Nitrodrome", Hope contacts The Looper again, saying that she found a second Pandora Gem at the Nitrodrome owned by Ringmaster Scarr. Scarr agrees to trade the gem in exchange for The Looper doing a magnificent stunt. After that is done Hope tells The Looper to retrieve and give her the gem. At the end of Part 3 of Story Quests, The Looper gives Hope the gem. Hope says that from her intel there are two Pandora Gems left to retrieve. Hope then tells The Looper that the gems show her visions, with one of those visions being of Megalo Don working with The Wanderer. She then tells the Looper to be aware of a person in weather-inappropriate robes. Meanwhile, Magneto arrives on the Island, breaking open the bunker near the Zenith Wall, which turns out to be the Weapon-X Secret Laboratory. In his dialogue, Magneto says that he has also seen The Wanderer and he describes them as an impending evil. In his quests he also tells us to configure three Rift Beacons around the map.

In Part 4, "Pea Bois Inc", Jones and Hope contact The Looper again, saying that the owner of the next Pandora Gem is Peabody, who, according to Jones' watchings, is not hostile. So Hope and Jones ask The Looper to talk to Peabody at Pea Bois HQ. He agrees to trade the gem for doing a few things for him. After helping propagate Peabody's Pea Buddies and testing tire bounciness, Peabody tasks The Looper to help his auto technician in training, Dummy, as a final request to get the gem. Dummy asks to collect some car parts since he can't work well because of the heat melting his plastic. With that complete, The Looper trades car parts to Peabody to receive the Pandora Gem. After doing so, Hope contacts them again, saying that there's just one gem left and it's with Megalo Don. At the end of Part 4 of Story Quests, The Looper gives Hope the gem. She says that her headache is stronger this time and she needs to relax, maybe paint something.

In Part 5, "The Great Leviathan Heist", Hope and Jones contact The Looper again, saying that the pipeline is almost finished and they need to get the last Pandora Gem quickly. After talking to Peabody, who reveals that the gem is in one of Megalo Don's caches across the Island, Jones and Hope ask The Looper to find the cache containing the last Pandora Gem. The Looper than proceeds to destroy objects at wasteland locations, eliminate wasteland guards, blow up vehicles, and damage players with vehicle weapons, bumpers, or Nitro Fists to find the gem. The Looper than managed to get a map, which shows the location of the Pandora Gem, from Megalo Don. Throughout all this Hope has more headaches from the gems. The Looper than obtains the final Pandora Gem from Megalo Don's hidden cache. At the end of Part 5 of Story Quests, The Looper gives the gem to Hope. All four Pandora Gems are put into the gem holder and The Oracle appears before Hope. The Oracle then says to Hope that they have a lot of talking to do. Meanwhile, the pipeline has been finished and Megalo Don's Leviathan ship will soon activate. Megalo Don is furious that Hope and Jones have stole all of his Pandora Gems and orders his marauders to bring Hope and Jones' heads to him. He also reveals that, when refueled, his Leviathan ship will "blacken the skies, rend the beaches in two, and DEVOUR your pathetic little cities!" and that he has been assured of this by a wise friend.

STORYLINE

In August 16th 2024 Chapter 5 Season 4 started.

War Machine, Shuri, Gwenpool, Iron Spider, Cyclops, Colossus, Jubilee, Cable, Rogue, and Gambit have arrived from Reality: 616. Peely has also been turned into Peelverine, Vengeance Jones became Captain Jones, and Hope is now Heroic Hope, who is destined to defeat Doom. Doom has also gone into hiding in order to make his new armour. In Part 1 of the Found Quests, Gwenpool had intentionally got "captured" in The Raft in order to get intel. The Looper steals an ID chip from a henchman and gives it to Gwenpool. Gwenpool says that the ID chip had *some* intel, but not all of it. She then asks The Looper to hack into The Raft's systems to get a flash drive using the passphrase "CYNTHIA", which they do and give to Gwenpool. Lastly, Gwenpool asks The Looper to steal some computer chips from electronic crates to give to Shuri. At the end of Part 1 of the Found Quests, The Looper gives the chips to Gwenpool. She mentions how Shuri needs the chips for "rift tech".

In Part 1 of Story Quests, "Are We Doomed?", The Looper finds Doom's helmet at Redline Rig, Hope, Jones, and The Oracle explain that the helmet is a prototype. The Looper proceeds to bring the helmet to Hope, who says that she's confused on how Doom took over the Island so quickly. She suspects that Doom is up to something that will destroy them all and she has no idea how to stop him. Jones says that Doom wanted them to find the helmet and has The Looper try to find anything else of importance with no success. The Looper than finds recording devices of Doom's memos, which reveal that Doom plans to make a world of perfect order and he needs to upgrade his armour in order to contain the box's power. At the end of Part 1 of Story Quests, Hope says that Doom is controlling his army remotely using Doombot technology and they need to first find Doom before we can fight him.

In Part 2 of Story Quests, "Heroes Assemble", Jones asks The Looper to open a Doom Chest. Jones asks them if there was anything interesting inside and starts telling them about his past. Later, The Looper eliminates a Latverian Guard to retrieve a shipping manifest from Doom. They give the manifesto to War Machine who deciphers it; it's pieces from Pandora's Box that Doom uses to create his new armour. So, The Looper gets trained by War Machine using his Arsenal and his Hover Jets. Later, they intercept a discussion between Jones, Hope and The Oracle, who says that she saw something new. "A feline ally. A cunning plan. A victory, hidden in defeat". Due to this, Hope panics more and more about her role as the heroine. At the end of Part 2 of Story Quests, Jones says that Shuri met with him, saying that she has a plan to take down the Doombot.

In Part 3 of Story Quests, "Shuri's Invention", Hope has The Looper go on a supply run via SHADOW Briefings while Shuri acquires some parts, including a power source, to reverse the energetic flow of the Rift Beacons, which Magneto used to summon superheroes from Reality: 616, to create Rift Recalibrators, which will hopefully Rift Doom off the Island. Once The Looper bring these parts to Shuri, she begins building the invention; meanwhile, Hope continues to doubt that Pandora's Box might have chosen her as the heroine wrongfully, despite the Oracle's prophecy, despite that, she will do her best to save the Island from Doom's domination. Later on, The Looper and Underground forces establish at least three Recalibrators near Castle Doom to test them on the Doombot. As the heroes engage the robot, the Recalibrators abruptly malfunctions when activated. This drives the Underground forces to destroy the Doombot and retreat as they hear Doom's voice threatening Hope that he is "judgement," even if she is chosen as the heroine as the prophecy foretold. Doom also began building a massive army of Doombots with Looper-based behaviours. Following the Underground's defeat, Hope and Shuri retrieve the data from the broken Rift Recalibrators, discovering that Doom's accomplices, Emma Frost and Mysterio, had sabotaged the devices, so the heroes must defeat them first before Doom arrives, starting with Mysterio.

In Part 4 of Story Quests, "The Illusionist", The Looper and Underground forces begin the hunt for Mysterio, starting by investigating his whereabouts and some mysteries in the peaceful-looking town of Doomstadt. According to the Oracle, the villain is conjuring illusions to brainwash many people of the Island, including the Wastelanders who sided with the Underground, this pushes Hope into liberating Mysterio's victims from his influence in which Jones remarks her passion for justice as an attribute of her leadership. The Underground forces eventually defeat the illusionist and vandalize Doomstadt through graffiti paintings and even deface the statue of Doctor Doom as a finishing touch. With Emma Frost as the next target, Shuri creates replacements of Rift Recalibrators to rift Doom off the Island. Although Hope still cannot understand if she has "everything [she] needs" to defeat him, the Oracle clarifies that Hope must seek prophetic enlightenment to help her accomplish her goal as a heroine.

In Part 5 of Story Quests, "I am Iron Man", Tony Stark, also known as Iron Man, has arrived on the island in his Mark 45 Armour, has recovered from his defeat by Doctor Doom, and is now providing Stark Tech, including energy rifles and his and War Machine's gear, via chests and Stark Mobile Armouries to the Underground forces; thus helping them turn the tide in the war. Later, while The Looper is flying above Castle Doom with Iron Man's Flight Kit, they guide the Rift Recalibrators to detect a strong signal above here. After the Underground forces test the firepower of Stark weaponry, they plan to eliminate Emma Frost with Tony's help. Meanwhile, at the start of "Stark Fan Club Quests", when The Looper hears about the Stark Fan Club from the Wastelanders, they head to Doomstadt to communicate with Super Stark Fan, via laptop, who aims to collect Stark memorabilia. After The Looper receives a coffee mug from Tony, his sunglasses found in one of Stark chests, and a dartboard with War Machine's support, they help complete the collection to gain the appeal of Stark Fan Club.

In Part 6 of Story Quests, "Diamond in The Raft", Hope subtly instructs The Looper on a plan to eliminate Emma Frost via phone to avoid the mutant's telepathy. With a help of a Forecast Tower and a hired Scout or Supply Specialist, the Underground forces proceed with the plan by planting Tony's power dampeners near the Raft to nullify Emma's psychic abilities, which provokes her. As The Looper fights Emma, suddenly, Jones warns Hope that the Rift Beacons have detected a big hostile signal heading towards the island, Doctor Doom has finished perfecting his armour and is on his way back. Although The Looper defeat Emma Frost, Hope continues to panic that they are not ready to face him. However, the Oracle mentions that heroes of prophecies care about justice in a right way, despite the fact they can "scream" and "cry", and she also noted that Hope's persistence and fighting spirit makes her the leader and the heroine; this helps Hope regain her confidence to win the war against Doom.

Before the Absolute Doom Event, Peelverine needs The Looper's help to activate the large Rift Beacons, placed at the west of Reckless Railways and pointing towards Doom's Arcane Forge. As they are brought online by Hope as the timer for the Live Event ticks down, the timer eventually hits zero and the Rift Beacons attempt to warp the Forge off the island with Doom in it.
