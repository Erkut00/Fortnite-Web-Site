STORYLINE

In December 1st 2024 Chapter 6 Season 1 started.

The artifact crash-lands on Oninoshima, producing a shockwave that re-awakens the Island's long-dormant magic. Portals scattered throughout the Island are re-activated, paving the way for demons to invade. Hope and Jones— who left his shield behind on Helios— settle in the city of Hopeful Heights to look for the artifact, which they discover has characteristics similar to Pandora's Box. In Part 1 of Story Quests, The Looper meets Oninoshima natives Kendo and Jade, joining them to help defend the Island against the demons. Jade meets Hope, who is eager to learn more about the Island's magic. The three collect essence from fallen demons and deliver it to Hope, allowing them to learn more about magic and the nature of the demons.

In Part 2, Jade goes missing, and Kendo believes his brother Daigo is responsible. Kendo discovers that Jade is trapped in the Spirit Realm from a portal in Nightshift Forest, and he sends The Looper to Masked Meadows to investigate Daigo's hidden underground workshop. There, The Looper finds that Daigo had given Jade one of his masks, which were crafted from dark magic using energy siphoned from trapped Sprites. When they confronted Daigo, he claims no ill will, stating that Jade got herself cursed by the demons and that the mask he gave her was meant to protect her, but it only made things worse. A puzzled Kendo notes that only demons can cross into the Spirit Realm.

In Part 3, Kendo investigates the artifact's crash site, only to find an empty crater. He and Hope find that splinters of the artifact had scattered across the Island, and Hope deduces that the demons are tied to them. Using the splinters, Kendo forges mystical Splinter Blades at Demon's Dojo before defeating the Puppeteer and momentarily releasing the Night Rose from her enslavement. He entrusts the Blades to her in hopes of saving Jade.

In Part 4, Hope investigates the crater and the mountains to examine remnants of the artifact's energy. The Looper collects demon essence from the demon leader Shogun X, and Hope discovers from it that the artifact is a Shard of the Zero Point. Meanwhile, the Night Rose had given the Splinter Blades to Daigo, who then retreated to his workshop and began trapping Sprites once more to craft six new masks using their energy. He then relocated to a temple south of Warrior's Watch with the masks, meditating outside a massive Demon Portal.
