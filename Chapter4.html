STORYLINE

In December 4th 2022 Chapter 4 Season 1 started.

The Ageless tasked Rift Warden Stellan to create a stable Rift Gate: a bridge to new realities. The Rift Gate is the Ageless' dearest wish. The Rift Warden also has his own reason to create it. There is a being: a Shapeless Man. He watches the Oathbound with dark purpose. If they don't finish the Rift Gate the consequences will be terrible. While Rift Warden Stellan is creating the Rift Gate, A.M.I.E does field research on the Rift Warden. A.M.I.E finds out that Stellan has officially harnessed inter-reality travel. A.M.I.E tries Stellan's Rift Gate. A.M.I.E embedded a bit of her code into those vessels and set up a test rift based on Stellan's design. The test rift is locked onto The Scientist and something horrible is happening to him. A.M.I.E needs to be ready for when the second Rift Gate goes online, to find The Scientist before it's too late.

Unfortunately, this could spell danger for the island. As far as we know, Rift Warden Stellan knows of The Nothing as he explains "there is a shapeless being." and opening the Rift Gate could lead us to the Chrome, possibly reviving The Herald in her tree form. As well as this, it could lead countless other realities to this one.

The Rift Warden came to the Oathbound with nothing, no family, no name. His work and his secrets have always been his own, until now. He came to the Oathbound at the order of the Shapeless Man. He yearns for a Rift Gate and if he fails... The Shapeless Man will destroy everything. Stellan cannot maintain the power needed to open the Rift Gate. He needs to advance the energy systems exponentially to handle this kind of demand. Raw Kinetic Ore, when undisturbed pulses with unimaginable power. Stellan captured enough energy to power the stars themselves. The energy is extremely unstable but The Shapeless Man is impatient. He must stabilize the energy long enough to power the Rift Gate. In order to channel the power into the Rift Gate he must forge a new alloy of Kinetic Ore. Stellan's dreams are filled with cold wind and The Shapeless Man's dark whispers. If the Rift Gate fails, Stellan fears his nightmares will devour his waking life. The Ageless Champion has set Stellan's coordinates and soon, Stellan will attempt to open the gate. A.M.I.E knows that if The Scientist is facing "a fate darker than the worst imaginable" she will need a body built for the other side of that Rift Gate. A.M.I.E's upgrades include a pocket chainsaw, flamethrowers, a little cheese knife, and a jazzy piano. She draws up blueprints, but the stiff winds blew the plans clean out of A.M.I.E's printer tray. A.M.I.E finds her blueprints and picks up some computer chips on page 7. It's a model of the chip inside of the Guardian Shields. A.M.I.E doesn't know what's happening to The Scientist but she will find him and bring him back to the Island. And maybe, when she does, The Scientist will look at everything she has done and he'll finally see her as his superior. A.M.I.E says her goodbyes to us.

The Rift Warden says that "This Rift Gate could tear a permanent hole in Reality". The Rift Warden longs to tell The Ageless the truth of the Shapeless Man, but he cannot. Each night the Rift Warden dreams of stars, swallowed in darkness and the Shapeless Man waiting. Stellan claims to have not dreamed of him for centuries. Stellan does not wish for us to succeed, but we will suffer if we fail. Stellan can't sleep. The Shapeless Man's gaze burns into Stellan. The Shapeless Man doesn't care if the Rift Gate tears the island in two. Stellan asks us to turn on the Rift Gate and if it is unstable, the stars will determine our fate. The Rift Gate will not stabilize and Stellan can't shut it off. Someone has manipulated the coordinates for the Rift Gate. Stellan asks us to find our saboteur, demand answers and must know what is coming!

Stellan says the energy of the Rift Gate is destabilizing, saying it could rip open the entire sky and they have no idea what's on the other side. The gate is destroyed, the sky is torn. Everything may be ending. He asks us to take telescopes to the place he marked and look at the sky. The stars will decide the end, but Stellan says "Thank you for everything, my friend."

STORYLINE

In March 10th 2023 Chapter 4 Season 2 started.

After The Looper hacks into an encrypted Arcade Cabinet that happened to contain Peace Syndicate servers, they are caught by Evie, impressed by their ability, to recruit them for a secretive mission. It is revealed that MEGA City is from Evie's home reality, and that the city is in danger. Evie introduces us to Thunder, her superior and high ranking member of the Peace Syndicate, who then has The Looper prove their combat abilities by shooting down various targets in MEGA City. Then, Evie tells us she has suspicions that a saboteur is trying to start a war between all of the syndicates, by messing with one and pinning the blame on another - in an attempt to cause complete chaos so they can take control of the city. The Peace Syndicate's data network is hacked, so for The Looper's first mission, Evie asks us to retrieve the stolen data. After investigating, a terminal is found containing all of the lost data, including documents on Peace Syndicate members, Thunder's family recipes, and various other documents that have been corrupted. Evie finds that all of the Peace Syndicate's intel on the army of the Last Reality was amongst the files corrupted by the Saboteur. Thunder warns us that if the Saboteur is working with The Last Reality there is more than an inter-syndicate war at stake

Evie is planning on dealing with the situation by uniting all of the syndicates together, so they decide to start with the River Guard. The River Guard are allies with the Peace Syndicate, and after gaining respect of The Looper they agree to take part in a safe meeting with their historic rivals, the Fox Clan. Despite their long-time hatred, the Fox Clan agree to take part in the meeting, so The Looper secured a meeting location for the two syndicates. To Evie's surprise, everyone remained calm and no weapons were drawn, with the meeting ending in an alliance between the River Guard and Fox Clan.

Evie later receives a message from The Luminaries that the saboteur has been stealing prototypes of weapons from their lab. Evie wants to recruit The Luminaries for her plan to bring peace to MEGA City, so she offers to find and return the weapon parts with The Looper in exchange for the Luminaries' assistance. After returning the weapon parts, The Luminaries are impressed and want The Looper to use their advanced technology to zero in on the location of the saboteur. After collecting crucial data with the Luminaries' sensor backpack, Evie, Thunder and the Looper report to Mystica. They reveal from the data that the saboteur is working with a syndicate called The Unseen that live for causing wild chaos, but it is unusual for them to do so quietly. Mystica says The Luminaries need time to investigate further. Later it's revealed that Dahlia was the saboteur and was working with The Last Reality and Triarch Nox. At the end of the season Earthquakes start happening across the Island, beginning the story for the next season.

STORYLINE

In June 9th 2023 Chapter 4 Season 3 started.

Slone, thought to be dead after the conclusion of the Zero War, found herself trapped in the jungle before or during Zero Fusion after she was rescued by someone. She no longer wears the Imagined Order insignia and tells Gunnar in character dialogue that she is "done" with the IO. It’s been revealed that Relik is her rescuer. When The Looper talks to her as Relik, she says, "Just because you got me back on my feet doesn't mean I owe you," which correlates to one of her audio logs where she says, "I’ll have to thank my rescuer. Hope he doesn’t expect a fruit basket." In another one of her five audio logs scattered around the jungle, Slone says that she’s "found an ancient busted machine" thought to be a "telescope or stellar projector." After getting closer to The Apparatus, Slone learns that it uses refined Kinetic Ore prisms to "manipulate light."

Elsewhere on the Island, Trace stumbles across a "powerful object" in the jungle they don’t understand. Treasure hunter Aura finds out and enlists The Looper to help her track down its location. She tells The Looper to steal field notes about the Chalice from The Explorers files. After reporting back to Aura, she says "The cup wanted to be found by a new wielder" so orders The Looper to investigate petroglyphs at Rumble Ruins to help locate the Chalice. The Looper returns the petroglyph sketches to Aura and from them, she recreates a map that reveals the Chalice of Cubetastrophe is located where Anvil Square once stood. Aura guides The Looper to the hidden ruins in the jungle to retrieve the Chalice. They offer 100 bars and collect the Chalice from Orelia's box. After having succeeded in finding the chalice and having taken it, The Looper transforms into Orelia (if female skin) or Oro (if male skin). The Looper returns to Aura as Orelia or Oro and they come to an agreement: Aura would take and seal the chalice and Orelia and Oro would be resettled in a golden fork. The Looper turns back to their normal form and Aura manages to seal the Chalice of Cubetastrophe before it falls into the wrong hands.

Optimus Prime tells us on the Fortnite radio station, that he has been in contact with Slone. Optimus Prime tells us that Slone alone knows the path forward. Optimus Prime warns us that great danger is on the horizon. The power that once flowed through the Island has faded, and formidable enemies have taken notice. Then Optimus proceeds to tell us that the answers we seek lie deep within the starless void that brought you here. Optimus tells us that those who lived on the Island long ago discovered a secret among the stars. Saying the artifacts they left behind will help us, and tells us we will find everything we need to study the stars.

Slone's investigation into the Apparatus have been interrupted by a group called the Explorers. Slone then makes contact with the Explorers as a possible expert for their team. Slone says that if she can get them to excavate the site she can get the Apparatus working. Slone looks at the glyphs as they are the only clues for the Apparatus. The Explorers have pushed on with the excavation. While Slone needs to find out how to power it. Slone moves from her tank over to Rumble Ruins and uncovers a strange glyph on the wall.

After the Loopers get all the prisms to Trace they tell us "You found it! The last Kinetic Prism we need. We've been working with Doctor Slone to get them installed for the Apparatus." After Doctor Slone activates the Apparatus, it gives a countdown until the Eclipse starts, tying in to the next season.

STORYLINE

In August 25th 2023 Chapter 4 Season 4 started.

Kado Thorne is brought to the Island; The Ancients were trying to warn Slone about this. However, Slone and the luminaries built the Apparatus. Kado Thorne is a time-traveling vampire, who brought his hotels and Guards to the Island. Kado uses his time machine to collect artifacts from different Fortnite seasons such as the Golden Egg, Mythic Goldfish, Midas' Drum Gun, The Foundation's MK-Seven Assault Rifle, Oro's Skull, as well as other items such as Kit's Shockwave Launcher.

Slone calls for someone called Nolan Chance who she thinks can help her steal the Time Machine from Kado Thorne. Antonia and Nolan Chance don't trust each other due to a previous heist, however they decide to team up. Nolan and The Loopers manage to get Fish Thicc, Piper Pace and Mae to help them join the crew for the Heist.

After a couple of months, The Eclipse began on the island, and Kado Thorne now has full power in his Vampire form, having acquired new abilities. He opened the passage to the Time Machine, protecting it and his previously stolen relics. He set the time machine to July 12 2018 but for what reason is unknown; Mae however suspects he has a much sinister plan in mind.

A little later, Doctor Slone, Nolan, and his crew successfully steal the Time Machine from Kado Thorne, stashing it in Frenzy Fields just outside of The Getaway. When they tested out the Time Machine; props from Chapter 1 appeared on the farm.

Agent Jones arrived on Asteria after finding his way back on the island after Fracture and now working with Doctor Slone to execute her plan. Near the end of the season the time machine and the props disappeared along with Agent Jones. In the last week of the season Kado Thorne was implied to be killed by Shimmerdusk. Despite this Asteria is still being affected by The Eclipse.
